You can send a coins to another address using the following instructions.

1) Open your QT wallet.
2) Click on the toolbar button "Send".
3) Enter the receiving address after "Pay To:".
4) Enter the amount of coins you want to send after "Amount:".
5) Click on the button "Send" to send the transaction.

Follow these additional steps when you see the message "Warning: Fee estimation is currently not possible."

6) Click on the toolbar button "Choose...".
7) Select "Custom".
