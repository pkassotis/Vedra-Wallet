Use the following instructions to restore a backup of your wallet.

Note: replace “examplecoin” with the name of your coin.

1) Close your wallet.
2) Open the folder “%APPDATA%\examplecoin\”.
3) Make an (extra) backup of the file “wallet.dat”.
4) Copy the file “wallet.dat” from your original backup to the folder “%APPDATA%\examplecoin\”.
