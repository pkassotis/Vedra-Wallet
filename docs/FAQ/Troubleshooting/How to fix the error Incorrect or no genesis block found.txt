The message “Incorrect or no genesis block found. Wrong datadir for network” is caused by a different blockchain with the same name.

Use the following instructions to fix this.

Note: replace “examplecoin” with the name of your coin.

1) Close your wallet.
2) Make a backup of the folder “%APPDATA%\examplecoin\”.
3) Remove the folder “%APPDATA%\examplecoin\”.
4) Start your wallet.
